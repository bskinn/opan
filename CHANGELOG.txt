Changelog for Open Anharmonic


v0.4rc1
===============================================================================

The v0.4 release is functionally complete, and is only missing some
User's Guide content.  Barring reporting of major bugs, v0.4rc1 will
become v0.4 once this content is drafted.

Classes/modules with no currently planned additional functionality:

* OpanXYZ
* OrcaEngrad
* OrcaHess
* utils.inertia

Classes/modules with some functionality, but planned augmentation/alteration:

* OrcaOutput
* utils.execute

Modules with stable functionality, but which will likely have future additions:

* const
* error
* utils
* utils.decorate
* utils.vector

Non-functional modules, with future implementation planned:

* utils.symm
* vpt2
